About me

Hi, my name is Flor Braz and I'm an UX designer and Front-end Developer based in Brazil.

I've been working in this area for 8 years now, dealing with IE6, IE7, and slow connections/PCs. Nowadays the web has never-ending possibilities and I'm willing to explore them all. My knowledge in UX design allows me to have a better understanding of the layouts I code and I’m often able to advise the creatives in what would be the best approach in a certain page or type of business.

Right now, I'm working at Itelios leading the front-end development team in Brazil, focusing in the architecture of new projects, code reviewing and training the team (and myself :P).

When I’m not working, I like to travel, getting to know new places and enjoying good meals (whether I’m cooking or going to restaurants) and good beers.
